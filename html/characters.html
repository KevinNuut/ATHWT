Families And Characters Of ATHWT
The show is written and staged like your every day soap opera, with the characters being broken up into three families. Each family has their own set of problems, however, their paths are forced to meet when money, greed, land, and drugs become involved.

The Wellington Family

Mr. Wellington
Mr. Wellington inherited his super fortune from his father, who created the robot race. He is in a continual struggle with the Chavez family for control of the town and with the Robot family for control of his finances.

Julie Wellington
Julie Wellington is a spoiled rich girl who thinks the world revolves around her...and in her father's eyes, it does. Recently, she has met the man of her dreams, in Rafe, and plans to marry him...with her father's approval.

Ginger
Following Mr. Wellington's first wife's tragic accident, he took Ginger as his trophy wife. He met Ginger in a cockfighting arena in Thailand and instantly fell in love with the fact that she has some of the largest breasts in existence and she is a mute.

Rafe
Rafe met Julie in college and began dating her. After a time, or a few times anyway, they fell in love and decided to get married. Rafe is a very normal guy with the exceptions of being a vegan and an alien of some kind.

Butler
Mr. Wellington's Butler has a thankless job. He waits on Mr. Wellington, hand and foot and beckons to his every insane request and order. However, Butler may be more than meets the eye...and that isn't a Transformer joke.

The Robot Family

Charles Robot
Charles is the father of the Robot family and was one of the first robots designed by Mr. Wellington's father. Because of this, he feels his family is entitled to his share of the Wellington fortune.

Nancy Robot
Nancy is the mother of the Robot family and is horribly addicted to drugs. This addiction has complete control of her and could end up destroying the entire Robot family.

Cindy Robot
Cindy is the daughter of the Robot family and has the typical problems a teenage girl has at that age, she weighs half a ton. Cindy is constantly fighting with her brother, Sam, and could easily be pushed into some of the darker areas of the family.

Sam Robot
Sam is the son of the Robot family and is a smart ass following in his father's foot steps of hating the Wellingtons. Sam loves to tease Cindy about her weight or anything else he can think of.

The Chavez Family

Maria Chavez
Maria is the mother and head of the Chavez drug cartel. Following the death of her husband, in a very unfortunate badger accident, which she had nothing to do with, Maria seized control of most of the drug operations in the county. She is constantly looking for ways to rid herself of Mr. Wellington and take complete control of the town.

Nina
Nina is Maria's right hand woman and enforcer of the Chavez drug cartel. She is responsible for all collections and expansions of the business and takes her role very seriously. She is also responsible for looking out for Maria's two moron sons, Roberto and Johnny.

Roberto Chavez
Roberto is the slightly smarter of Maria's twin sons. He is constantly trying to make his mom happy by doing what he thinks she would want, but all he ends up doing is messing things up even worse.

Johnny Chavez
Johnny is the incredibly dumber of Maria's twin sons. His constantly trying to stay focused on Roberto's plans with so many shiny things around. He loves to eat at Pizzapotamus and play skeeball.
