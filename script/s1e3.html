INTERIOR CHAVEZ COMPOUND BASEMENT

MARIA, the mother and leader of the Chavez family, stands in the basement of her elaborate compound. She is a muscular red head with a large metal arm. She's cold blooded and only has a weak spot for money and her retarded sons, Roberto and Johnny.

Maria aims a harpoon gun to an unseen area. She fires three shots.

NINA enters from behind Maria. Nina is Maria's assistant and serves as the strong arm of the Chavez drug cartel. She is a blond girl with a welder's shield pulled up on top of her head.

NINA
You're getting pretty good with that thing.

MARIA
Is everything going to plan?

NINA
Yes, Ms. Chavez.

MARIA
Excellent.
(beat)
That moron, Wellington, won't know what hit him.

Maria fires some shots and turns to Nina.

MARIA (CONT'D)
My husband may have been a well respected member of the community, but what he lacked was vision... Initiative.

Maria fires another shot.

MARIA (CONT'D)
And testicles. But that was mostly my doing.
(beat)
Well mostly the badger's doing. But I bought it...and fed it.

Maria laughs sadistically for an awkward amount of time. She slowly stops.

MARIA (CONT'D)
Was that too evil?

NINA
No. No. It was fine.

MARIA
Are you sure? Cause it felt a little...over the top.

NINA
The laugh might have been a bit much.

Maria looks away from Nina and fires two more shots. She looks back and Nina.

NINA (CONT'D)
But that's just one person's opinion. Not mine, but one... person's.

MARIA
Uh huh.
(beat)
How have the local sales been going?

NINA
Pretty good.
(beat)
Except for-

Maria stops firing and looks at Nina.

MARIA
What?

NINA
That robot in Bunker 62 still owes us. Big.

MARIA
How big?

NINA
Small nation fighting for its independence against a ruthless warlord hell bent on world domination size.

MARIA
Take care of that. Personally.

Nina nods, turns, and walks out. Maria fires two more shots.

MARIA (CONT'D)
Now, are you gonna try to steal my newspaper again?

A MAN with dozens of harpoons stuck into his body. His voice trembles.

MAN
Sorry.

MARIA
I'm glad we could have this talk. You can go now.

The Man walks past Maria.

MARIA (CONT'D)
And tell your wife I want to talk to her about her rose garden.

The Man nods and runs out. Maria stares into the distance.
