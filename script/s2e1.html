INTERIOR BUNKER MAIN ROOM

Charles stands in the main room of the bunker looking up at the charts on the wall.

Cindy enters the room and stands behind Charles.

CINDY
Dad?

Charles keeps looking at the charts on the wall.

CHARLES
Yes, honey.

CINDY
What’s an eight ball?

CHARLES
It’s the black ball on a pool table.

CINDY
Oh.
(beat)
Okay.

Charles continues staring at the charts on the wall. Cindy does not move from her spot.

CINDY (CONT’D)
Dad?

CHARLES
Yeah, cupcake.

CINDY
What does “white meat” mean?

CHARLES
It’s a type of chicken, honey.

CINDY
Oh.
(beat)
Then why would some one cut chicken if they didn’t get the money for the pool balls?

Charles turns around.

CHARLES
What are you talking about?

CINDY
Well I was upstairs and some guys were moving all this chemistry stuff into the rumpus room.

CHARLES
What?

CINDY
And I heard this guy tell this other guy that if he didn’t get the money for the eightballs he would cut him to the white meat.

Charles whips toward the door.

CHARLES
Nancy! Get in here!

CINDY
I’m going to go get some of that chicken from that guy.

Nancy enters the room. Charles turns to Cindy.

CHARLES
Leave them alone and go get your brother for dinner.

Cindy leaves as Nancy joins the conversation.

CHARLES
Nancy, why are there men upstairs making and selling drugs?

NANCY
They told me they were avant-garde Lego artists who needed a space to to express their bold and controversial designs.

CHARLES
Nancy!

NANCY
I gave half the house to the Chavez family.

CHARLES
You did what?!

NANCY
I had to. I owed so much money to them. They threatened my life.

CHARLES
You gave them our house?

NANCY
Only half. We still have somewhere to live. As long as we don’t go upstairs. That’s considered off limits now. And don’t even try to steal any drugs unless you like box cutters to the eye.

CHARLES
I can’t believe you did this to us.

NANCY
Personally, I don’t like box cutters… but I do like the drugs.

CHARLES
Focus, Nancy! So you don’t owe them anything else? This is it, right?

NANCY
Well…not exactly. Half of the house was half of the debt.

CHARLES
What was the other half?

Cindy re-enters the room.

CINDY
Dad, I can’t find Sam anywhere. Can I have his dinner?

Charles turns from Cindy to Nancy.

CHARLES
What did you do?
